About me

Hello there! My name is Jorge Rodriguez and I'm a Level Designer. I graduated from college in video game design, and after that, I spent around two years working on my own indie game with some college friends. Right after the game launched, I started working as a Level designer in Nimble Giant.

Video games have been my passion since I was a kid, and that hasn't changed since then. I play all kinds of games but, at the moment, I'd say my favorites are probably RPGs and Immersive Sims.

Outside video games, I'm especially interested in reading and writing. I'm a big fan of fantasy, horror, and sci-fi novels but, in general, I'm particularly interested in stories that focus on characters.

Whenever I can, I always try to find new stuff to learn about and try for myself (especially if it gives me an excuse to step out of the computer). Lately, I've been into baking bread and doing more crafty stuff, mainly assembling and painting gundams and miniatures.

Down here, you can see and download my CV. If you need anything else, feel free to contact me at jorgerodriguezdev@gmail.com.
