GAME SUMMARY

Ignited Steel it's a rogue-like tactics game with a sci-fi setting. The core idea of the game is the managing of the mech's temperature. The player controls a squad of old mechs with overheating issues fighting against evil AI that has rendered useless current technology.
Overheating the mechs is a double edged sword. The greater the heat, the greater the damage both dealt and received.

Out of combat, the player will be able to modify each mech's weapon, support equipment, and core, modifying their attributes and their available actions. In their journey, the player will not only fight but also encounter themselves in unexpected events that will lead to different situations in every run.

The game was realeased in March 9, 2022 and some months later the team released a free prologue with new extra content (I only worked partially in the update as I was already working at Nimble Giant at the time)
