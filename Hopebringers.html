GAME SUMMARY

Hopebringers is a tactics game with a medieval fantasy setting. The main idea of the game was to have a tactics game with a tabletop game feeling and almost no randomness. The player has 8 characters with different skills and upgrades. Each character has a unique skill set and utilities. For example: the knight doesn't receive damage from the front and, its upgrades revolve around the use of his shield, being able to upgrade its defenses or use it as a weapon to push enemies.

The game consists of 25 levels and 3 extra random generated levels. Between each level, the characters will have conversations advancing the plot forward.
